# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the BaseCompose community, including but not limited to maintainers and contributors, will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Report any violations to the maintainers

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Violent threats or language directed against another person
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors

## Reporting Issues

If you believe someone is violating this code of conduct, please report it by emailing the maintainers. All reports will be kept confidential and will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
